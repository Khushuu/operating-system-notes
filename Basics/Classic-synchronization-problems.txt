Producer-Consumer problem (Bounded-buffer)
	- producer generates data and keeps in BB 
	- consumer retrieves data from buffer for processing 
	- producer must not add data when buffer is full and consumer must not retrieve data when buffer is empty 
	- GOAL: 
		synchronize producer and consumer operations to ensure data consistency and prevent buffer overflows 	and underflows
	- SOLUTION: 
		Use Semaphores
		- mutex semaphore: for mutual exclusion 
		- empty semaphore: to track empty slots in buffer 
		- full semaphore: to track filled slots in buffer
